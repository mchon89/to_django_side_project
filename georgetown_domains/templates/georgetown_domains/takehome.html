STORY: 1970 vs 2016

There has been a general pattern of terror attacks happening around the world that the number of people killed due to terrorism increases gradually as time passes by. Especially, compared to 1970, 2016 is much more dangerous and difficult for innocent people to avoid terror attacks because terror attacks are ubiquitous and armed with more destructive, weapons which enable terrorists to kill more people easily and without a hassle. The story here is trying to tell how terror attacks are different from 1970 to 2016.
